## Top Requests by Frequency

### 1. Instagram Visibility & Social Media (Most Requested)

**What vendors want:**
- More Instagram posts featuring their work
- Consistent tagging of all vendors in posts
- Vendor spotlights on social media
- Collaborative posts (multiple vendors tagged)
- Story features and highlights
- Instagram posts as slide/carousel stories (storytelling format)
- Easy-to-share content
- More Pinterest presence
- TikTok sponsored posts

**Specific quotes:**
- "Instagram visibility, so any kind of publications including mentioning Wedding Vendors are much appreciated!"
- "Partager leur travail et leur nom sur instagram" (Share their work and name on Instagram)
- "Always more posts on Instagram :)"
- "I particularly enjoy when Instagram posts are designed as slides that tell the story of a wedding"

**Priority**: HIGH - This is the #1 most frequent request

---

### 2. Vendor Directory Enhancements

**What vendors want:**
- **SEO-optimized listings** with better search visibility
- **Searchable/filterable directory** by:
  - Region/location
  - Style/aesthetic
  - Service type
  - Budget range
- **Profile management access** for vendors to update their own listings
- **Performance insights**: views, leads, inquiries, conversions
- **Interactive features**: real-time inquiries, direct contact options
- **Regional coverage clarity**: Show which regions vendors serve
- **Visual improvements**: More photos, better presentation

**Specific quotes:**
- "It would be interesting to know how to get on the vendors list on your website"
- "A searchable vendor directory with filters (by location, aesthetic, services offered)"
- "Clearer performance insights (views, leads, conversions) to help vendors maximize their presence"
- "Creating an interactive map where vendors are based to help couples to plan their destination wedding"
- "Maybe showing that we cover more regions"

**Priority**: HIGH - Directory subscriptions = primary revenue

---

### 3. Communication & Transparency

**What they LOVE (keep doing):**
- ✅ Quick email responses ("I received an email response quickly, which is so nice these days")
- ✅ Available and responsive ("you respond very quickly and are really available for vendors")
- ✅ Clear, fluid communication

**What they want MORE of:**
- **Publication notifications**: Let vendors know when their feature goes live
- **Publication timelines**: Expected publish dates
- **Submission feedback**: Even if rejected, provide quick feedback
- **Clear submission guidelines**: What you're looking for aesthetically, image formats, caption styles
- **Submission process simplification**: Make it easier to submit
- **Performance metrics post-publication**: Readership, engagement stats

**Specific quotes:**
- "Quick feedback, even if it's a no, would also be appreciated"
- "It's nice to get a notification of when the wedding gets published"
- "Clearer submission guidelines and visual references would be helpful"
- "Transparency around publication timelines and sharing performance insights"

**Priority**: MEDIUM - Operational improvement, enhances vendor experience

## Prioritization Framework

### Immediate Wins (High Impact, Low Effort)
1. **Consistent vendor tagging** on Instagram posts
2. **Publication notifications** when features go live
3. **Submission guidelines page** with clear aesthetic examples
4. **"Featured by FWS" badge** for vendors to use
5. **More Instagram carousel posts** showcasing vendor work

### Short-Term (3-6 months)
1. **Vendor spotlight series** (weekly/monthly featured vendor)
2. **Tips from vendors content series** (social + blog)
3. **Vendor newsletter** (monthly trends, opportunities, tips)
4. **Enhanced directory filters** (region, style, service type)
5. **Shareable content templates** for vendors

### Medium-Term (6-12 months)
1. **Vendor profile management portal** (self-service updates)
2. **Performance insights dashboard** (views, inquiries, engagement)
3. **Regional vendor roundups** ("Top 10 Provence wedding photographers")
4. **Collaborative styled shoot program**
5. **Vendor community platform** (WhatsApp/Discord group)

### Long-Term (12+ months)
1. **Annual FWS Awards or event**
2. **Educational workshop series** (masterclasses)
3. **Premium membership tiers** with enhanced features
4. **Lead generation platform** (real-time inquiries, couple matching)
5. **Physical print magazine**

## Notes on Capacity & Resources

As a founder-led operation, prioritize initiatives that:
- **Scale with automation** (e.g., badges, templates, guidelines)
- **Leverage existing content** (e.g., vendor spotlights from real wedding features)
- **Enhance revenue** (directory subscriptions, premium tiers)
- **Strengthen vendor relationships** without overwhelming bandwidth
- **Can be batched** (monthly vendor newsletter vs. daily tasks)

**Remember**: Vendors already love what you do. Improvements should enhance, not overhaul.
